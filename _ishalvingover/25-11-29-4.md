"Disgusting, disgusting was the outside of the dome. The contrary-being-ness has the limit -- below that line corrupt my mind. Unfortunate is that not all parts are of such degenerate degree -- but they're not contained... So I prefer staying inside here. That's why I try," said he to his lover of the castle. "They that're *above* us... I hope they cleaned them out first before damaging our dome more."

Instead of such grim stories, he wanted to think something else, to purify his mind. The girl of the town. That orange scene collaped into a set of horizontal lines, then again to points, then to one point -- then he heard her replying to what he said lying on his bed. "Yeah, yeah;" he needed more sleep.
